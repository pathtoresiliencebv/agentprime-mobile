## Customer Support FAQs

### What is AgentPrime?
AgentPrime is your super AI worker that creates real deliverables like AI slides, spreadsheets, documents, reports, and images – transforming how you work.

### What can I create with AgentPrime?
You can create AI-powered presentations, spreadsheets, documents, images, research reports, and more. AgentPrime also integrates with your favorite apps (Gmail, Google Drive, Notion, Slack, and many others) so it can perform actions directly in them – not just create content, but actually work in your apps.

### How is AgentPrime different from ChatGPT or other AI chatbots?
While most AI tools focus on conversation, AgentPrime creates real deliverables. Need a presentation? AgentPrime creates slides. Need data analysis? AgentPrime creates spreadsheets. Need research? AgentPrime creates reports. It's AI that delivers finished work products, not just responses.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. All conversations are encrypted, we never sell your data, and we're compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### What AI models does AgentPrime use?
AgentPrime supports multiple AI models including Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 (OpenAI), and others, giving you access to the most advanced AI capabilities available.

### Can I use AgentPrime for free?
Yes! AgentPrime offers a free plan with basic features. Pro and Team plans unlock advanced capabilities, unlimited creations, and higher usage limits.

### What apps can AgentPrime integrate with?
AgentPrime integrates with popular productivity apps including Gmail, Google Drive, Outlook, Notion, Airtable, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, Trello, Asana, GitHub, and many more. Once connected, AgentPrime can perform actions directly in these apps – sending emails, creating documents, updating databases, scheduling meetings, and more.

### What file formats can AgentPrime create?
AgentPrime can export to PowerPoint, Word, Excel, PDF, Google Slides, Google Docs, Google Sheets, and various image formats – whatever you need for your workflow.

### Does AgentPrime work offline?
You can view your created work offline, but creating new slides, documents, and reports requires an internet connection as AgentPrime uses advanced AI models in the cloud.

### Can I use AgentPrime on multiple devices?
Yes! All your created work – slides, documents, spreadsheets, and reports – syncs seamlessly across all devices through your AgentPrime account.

### How can I get help?
Visit support@kortix.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
